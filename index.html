Problems in Education System

The lack of practical experiences, insufficient resources, and focusing upon memorization instead of understanding are common issues in the current education system. In most of the schools, the teacher is not able to provide a practical demonstration of concepts due to unavailability of resources. Students interested in a particular topic are not able to get enough resources and information from the schools/teachers so that they could grow and become better in the skills they are interested in. Nowadays, there is more emphasis on rote memorisation and an unhealthy environment is created in schools where students study only to get more marks.

Problems with Students

  • Lack of Conceptual Learning and Visualization:
    Students are not made to visualize or imagine what they are being taught and they may struggle to understand complex concepts explained in class.
  • Non-Interactive Environment in Class:
    Due to communication and a skillset barrier between students and teachers
  • Less Innovation and Support:
    Both teachers and students need more resources for a proper explanation of concepts and for exploring their interests respectively.
  • Less Exposure:
    Students are given very low to zero opportunities by the schools to interact with their environment and the society around them.
  • Lack Of Efficiency:
    Students' time and energy expenditure does not give appropriate results and they have to provide extra time for tuition, coaching, etc.
  • No Profile for Students in their Community:
    There is no way a student can know where he stands or what his skill status is among the students of his same age group.
2 / 4

Problems with Teachers

  • Lack of Objects and Models for Teaching:
    Only theory-based teaching is done in classes, no physical/practical models are made available to the teachers.
  • Lack of Equipment and Setups for Experimentation
  • Lack of Time for designing well-planned Creative Class:
    The teachers are restricted by time-constraint from completing their own syllabi.
  • Lack of Resources (Customizable and Non-Customizable)
  • Lack of Support(Independent Support):
    There is no service available to the teachers to provide unbound support to them, assisting them in teaching in a more innovative and creative manner.
  • No Profile for teachers in the community:
    The teachers are unable to know their status or where they stand on the basis of their skills in their particular city or region.
3 / 4

Problems with Schools

  • Improper Tech Upgradation:
    Many schools are still dependent upon 2-D learning or chalk-blackboard learning.
  • No track of Class Structure:
    Syllabus and curriculum in schools are many times lagging behind because of no real-time track of concepts and topics covered on a regular basis in classes.
  • No awareness of the School's overall profile in their Town/City/State:
    There is no such system that provides a ranking of schools on the basis of various academic and non-academic parameters.
  • Lack of Network:
    Schools are not having any kind of interaction and communication with each other, making them isolated in their own Towns/Cities.
  • Load of academic work which makes conducting non-academic activities less frequent
  • Restricted Management:
    Schools have to manage their own academic tasks and conduct various activities within the premises, which makes organizing and participation in inter-school activities difficult.
  • Lack of Cross-Platform Learning:
    There is no flow of knowledge from one school to another due to no room for interaction between teachers from different schools.
4 / 4

Problems with Boards and Goverment

  • Lack of Network:
    Many schools are still dependent upon 2-D learning or chalk-blackboard learning.
  • No direct reach to the teachers and students:
    The point of contact is the school itself and there is no such system of communication for the students and the teachers.
  • No proper tracing and data analysis of the students and teachers:
    The boards as well as the government are unable to track down the talents or the skill levels of the students/teachers of a particular town/city/state because of the lack of a system for such analysis.
  • Untraceable data for different pools (skills, subjective, etc.):
    It is hard to know if the students or teachers in a particular area /region are better than those in some other area or region because there is no reliable method to measure and compare the skill levels.

Skiome Application
The Skiome app is basically made to implement all of its functionalities and services to the schools. It provides various facilities to the schools:
  • It provides an interface where teachers can purchase any object, model, or experiment required for their subject/topic in the vicinity of their homes through the application.
  • The teachers are also free to request any modifications/changes in the objects or the models they receive, providing them with an independent and personalized teaching experience.
  • The app contains all the models and objects for the entire senior and Senior-secondary classes. This data is divided into Categories which are a subjectwise division of the classes. Each category contains kits relating to all the concepts covered in that particular subject in that particular class. Each kit contains all the objects and setups, information related to them, the concepts covered, and a step-by-step guide on how to use them (along with a video demonstration).
  • It displays updates about the upcoming and ongoing events and workshops being organized by Skiome in the nearby regions of the school.
  • The app gives updates about the upcoming inter-school or inter-state competitions being held and further details about participating in them.
  • The app contains a profile about the schools and teachers associated with it and related information.
  • Achievements of students in particular fields or competitions are also maintained within the app.
  • Information about all the clubs, their structure and functionality, their specific organized events and competitions and their respective winners, etc, and related data is maintained within the app.

Skiome Center

Skiome Centre is a physically well-maintained and well-developed store that acts as a capital for all the offline and on-ground operations and services provided by Skiome. It stores all the objects, models, and experiments which are included within the Skiome application.

All the tech support and resource distribution demanded by the teachers, and customisation in the objects and models are done here within the centres. It works as a connecting point for all the schools fetching all their demands and providing Skiome features. Skiome centre will be having a centre in charge who will be responsible for all the functioning. Schools and teachers are free to contact the centre in charge for any query or help. Verified teachers and school authorities along with students will be allowed to visit the centre as and when specified or scheduled by the centre in charge. This also follows for the common public but there will be a restriction for them to get into the centre.
